About the Event

Join industry leaders, researchers, and policymakers for a comprehensive exploration of the next frontier in artificial intelligence. This year's conference focuses on ethical frameworks, generative models, and the socio-economic impacts of AI deployment across emerging markets.

Attendees will participate in hands-on workshops, engaging panel discussions, and networking sessions designed to foster collaboration and innovation. We will delve deep into how AI can be leveraged to solve critical regional challenges while maintaining robust ethical standards.
